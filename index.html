Financial Analysis of US Companies

In the 'Financial Analysis of US Companies' project, I conducted comprehensive analysis to evaluate the financial health, operational efficiency, and strategic positioning of multiple US companies. Utilizing Power BI, Navigation page, and Slicer, I cleaned data and implemented a manual date column to enhance analysis. By utilizing a star schema for clear data structure, I analyzed various categories including market position, risk management, cash flow, employee productivity, and financial performance. The outcome of the analysis identified AAPL as the strongest overall financially, and visualized company strengths and weaknesses to facilitate targeted improvement efforts.
